Charles Darwin

Main facts about Charles Darwin:

  • 1809 - Charles Darwin, an English naturalist, geologist, biologist, was born
  • 1838 - invented his theory of natural selection
  • 1859 - “On The Origin Of Species” was published, which has influenced on the theory of natural selection
  • 1871 - he examined human evolution and sexual selection in "The Descent of Man", and "Selection in Relation to Sex"
  • 1872 - "The Expression of the Emotions in Man and Animals"
  • 1881 - "The Formation of Vegetable Mould, through the Actions of Worms"
  • 1859 - "On the Origin of Species"
